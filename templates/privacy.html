Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 03, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Piráti Calendar Importer (the "Application") collects, uses, and discloses your information. The Application is a tool created by Filip Varga ("Developer") to help users import hockey match schedules into their personal Google Calendars.

2. Data We Collect

When you use the Application, we access the following data via your Google Account:

  • Google User Profile: We access your email address and basic profile information (OpenID) solely to identify your active session.
  • Google Calendar Data: We request access to write/edit events in your Google Calendar (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: Your email is used to create a temporary login session so you can access the tool.
  • Functionality: We use the Calendar permission strictly to insert hockey match events that you explicitly select. We do not read, delete, or modify your existing personal events.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • No Permanent Storage: This Application does not use a database. Your user data and access tokens are stored only in a temporary browser session ($_SESSION).
  • Session Data: Once you close your browser or click "Logout," your data is removed from the Application's memory.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties. Data is transferred directly between your browser, our server, and the Google API to perform the requested actions.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke the Application's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting the Google Security Settings.
